Welcome to Kobold Adventure!

Kobold Adventure is an adult, text-based adventure game. You will assume the role of a kobold, and try to survive in a hostile medieval fantasy world. You will face many trials and tribulations before the end, and your life will not be an easy one. Can you make it out alive, or will you fall prey to the many dangers of this world?

If you are unaware of how to play Kobold Adventure, you may click the help button at the bottom left of the game pane, which will open a comprehensive manual in a separate browser tab explaining everything you need to know to make it through your first day.

If playing Kobold Adventures has given you the urge to write your own scenes, a thorough guide on how to contribute to the game can be found by clicking the contribute button. The creator of Kobold Adventures went through great lengths to make sure that writing scenes is as easy as it can be, so don't hesitate to turn your idea into something that everyone can enjoy. The only requirements are proper knowledge of the English language and willingness to learn. Writing, website making or JavaScript programming skills are optional, but they will definitely ease the transition from having a great idea to being the proud writer of a scene in Kobold Adventure.

If you encounter any bugs, typos or oddities, don't hesitate to report them to the author of Kobold Adventure, TinkeringTurian. He is reachable via four different channels:

  • First and foremost, if you have a Github account, you may report issues via Kobold Adventure's public repository, available at https://github.com/GarrusVakarian/GarrusVakarian.github.io. This is the preferred method of issue reporting.
