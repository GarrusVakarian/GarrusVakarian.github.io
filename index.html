Welcome to Kobold Adventure!

Kobold Adventure is an adult, text-based adventure game. You will assume the role of a kobold, and try to survive in a hostile medieval fantasy world. You will face many trials and tribulations before the end, and your life will not be an easy one. Can you make it out alive, or will you fall prey to the many dangers of this world?

In order to get the best experience out of Kobold Adventure, it is recommended that you play using a webkit browser, such as Google Chrome. While the game will run on most if not all browsers, there is no guarantee that all features will be enabled. A very clear cut example of this is the custom scrollbar of the main game panel. If you are currently using a non-webkit browser, such as Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox or Internet Explorer, you will most likely have an ugly default one.

If you are unaware of how to play Kobold Adventure, you may click the help button at the bottom left of the game pane, which will open a comprehensive manual in a separate browser tab explaining everything you need to know to make it through your first day.

If playing Kobold Adventure has given you the urge to write your own scenes, a thorough guide on how to contribute to the game can be found by clicking the contribute button. The creator of Kobold Adventure went through great lengths to make sure that writing scenes is as easy as it can be, so don't hesitate to turn your idea into something that everyone can enjoy. The only requirements are proper knowledge of the English language and willingness to learn. Writing, website making or JavaScript programming skills are optional, but they will definitely ease the transition from having a great idea to being the proud writer of a scene in Kobold Adventure.

If you encounter any bugs, typos or oddities, don't hesitate to report them to the author of Kobold Adventure, TinkeringTurian. He is reachable via four different channels:

  • First and foremost, if you have a Github account, you may report issues via Kobold Adventure's public repository, available at https://github.com/GarrusVakarian/GarrusVakarian.github.io. This is the preferred method of issue reporting. Issues reported here are far easier to track, delegate and give status updates on than those reported via other channels. All of this doesn't mean that it's the only way to report stuff, though.
  • Secondly, if you have a Steam account, you may report issues directly to TinkeringTurian himself. His profile is available at http://steamcommunity.com/id/TinkeringTurian/". He'll be online on Steam most of the time he is awake, unless otherwise occupied. Send him a friend request, and he'll usually add you immediately. In case he missed your request, or if he's currently unavailable, you may also leave a comment on his profile, and he'll get back to you as soon as he can.
  • Thirdly, if you have a Patreon account, you may report issues by posting about them on Kobold Adventure's Patreon page, available at https://www.patreon.com/TinkeringTurian. Note that this is a very unreliable way of issue reporting, and should generally only be used if no other option is available.
  • Last, but not least, good old fashioned emailing is always an option. Definitely not the fastest way to report an issue, yet a lot more reliable than reporting via Steam or Patreon. The email address you should send to is: tinkeringturian@gmail.com. This address will be checked about once or twice daily. This is also where you should send all your fanmail.
